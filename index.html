What this version covers

This app uses simplified claiming math for retirement, delayed credits, a spousal top-up estimate, and survivor step-up logic. It is useful for personal comparisons, but it does not yet model earnings tests, taxes, deemed filing edge cases, restricted applications, government pension offsets, or exact SSA month-by-month calculations.

Survivor benefit comparison

Monthly survivor income across strategies, for each scenario.

How survivor benefits work (simplified): When one spouse passes, the surviving spouse keeps the larger of their own benefit or the deceased spouse's benefit. Delaying the higher earner's claim generally increases the survivor benefit because the deceased's delayed credits carry over. This tool estimates the step-up; actual survivor calculations may differ based on SSA rules.
